Information

For families with children and for snowboarders the ski-area Kötschach-Mauthen is made for you. The ski area offers good conditions for both beginners and experts of the white sport.

The ski resort Kötschach-Mauthen - Vorhegg is located in the Nassfeld-Pressegger See region (Austria, Carinthia). There are 6 km of slopes available for skiing and snowboarding. 4 lifts transport the guests. The winter sports area is located at an altitude of 710 to 1,300 m.

The ski resort Kötschach-Mauthen is a small ski area at the transition between Gailtal and Lesachtal in Carinthia. The region is known for sometimes being blessed with an abundance of snow. The slopes in Kötschach-Mauthen are made to practice and enjoy skiing and snowboarding comfortably with the whole family. Whether on the practice slope with platter lift or the slopes with double chair and drag lift: everyone will find the right descent here. In between, there is the possibility to recharge your batteries for a long, sunny skiing day with a stop at the Ödenhütte.

A relaxing end is offered by the "Aquarena " - water and wellness oasis, located directly at the valley station of the Vorhegg double chairlift.

For those who prefer a more leisurely pace
Cross-country skiers are awaited by over 52 km of double-tracked trails in all degrees of difficulty.
