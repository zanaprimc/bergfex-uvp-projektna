Information

A ski area ideal for families

We provide our skiers, wide, well-groomed and snowy slopes. The funny playground with air toys, tubing and tobogganing guarantes absolute fun for kids.

To the valley and mountain stations our guests have beautiful sun terraces, for those who are looking for a little rest and relaxation after or during a hard day's skiing.
