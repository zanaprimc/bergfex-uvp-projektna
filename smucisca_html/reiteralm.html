Reiteralm – cable car system:

“Silver Jet” 8-cabin cable car system on the Reiteralm, the most powerful cable railway in the Schladming region, with no long queues, providing ideal access to ultimate skiing enjoyment with 123 kilometres of slopes!
*Escalator, information centre, Silver Lounge with sofas and hot beverages, cosy, heated lounge area with leather sofas and free TV, including snacks and a vending machine.
*Modern ski depot with heated, lockable ski and shoe lockers.
*Large intersport centre – à la carte rental service located directly by the Silver Jet
*Children's paradise on the Reiteralm…with magic carpet conveyer, kid’s roundabout, fairy-tale characters and children's ski routes.
*The crème de la crème of the longest slopes - Finale Grande – a wonderful slope up to 80 m wide, located directly next to the new Silver Jet cable car system.

  • Recommended ski-run: Reiteralm round trip – the ultimate 6 km downhill experience! Starting point: Gasselhöh top station - Holzer carving slopes - Reiteralm ski-run and Finale Grande.

*****
Reiteralm mountain rail- and cableways – join the fun in the Ski Amade!
Unlimited skiing pleasure in the following regions: Schladming sporting region, Salzburger sporting world, Hochkönig, Großarl and Gastein.
*****
Reiteralm – with optimum access to Schladming’s ski carousel with its four main mountains! Sporty skiing pleasure over 123 km of wonderfully groomed ski-runs, accessed by 50 modern cable cars and lifts.
Visit the group of top ski peaks, REITERALM - HOCHWURZEN - PLANAI - HAUSER KAIBLING and look forward to a “warm” welcome!
*****
The REITERALM is the training peak for the international ski elite!
The elite members of the ÖSV (Austrian Ski Association) train on the Reiteralm alongside numerous international World Cup ski teams every ski season! The champions of the Olympics, the World Championships and the World Cup Championships fully appreciate the Reiteralm’s exclusive selection of groomed slopes…
