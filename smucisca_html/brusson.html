Information

Brusson is a rather well-known ski center, and guests international competitions for the disciplines of Nordic skiing and biathlon. The conformation of the land can satisfy different targets: families, children but also expert skiers. The landscape is completed by a rich nature and perfectly preserved traditional villages. The area around the slopes is very welcoming and is a loved destination for some relaxing sunbathing and panorama. Do not miss a visit to the historic gold mines of Tchamousira.
