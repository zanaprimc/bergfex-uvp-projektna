Information

  • Welcome to the Skiwelt Schöneck! *

We wish all winter friends pleasant hours in the Skiwelt Schöneck. Experience the finest skiing fun with slopes of all levels of difficulty, cross-country trails through the winter forest or tobogganing fun. Have fun in the Skiwelt Schöneck, the first certified family-friendly ski area in Saxony!

Innovations

Skiwelt Schöneck

The opening times of the Skiwelt Schöneck in the new ski season 2023/2024 remain unchanged from the previous season. The large four-person chairlift is open from Tuesday to Sunday from 9 a.m. to 6 p.m. and on Mondays until 4 p.m. You can ski for 12 hours on Fridays, because the four-person chairlift is open until 9 p.m.

Further information is available at www.schoeneck-vogtland.de
