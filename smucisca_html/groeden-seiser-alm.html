Information

Amazing ski slopes and enchanting views

Not only the expanse of Europe's largest high alpine pasture and the breathtaking backdrop of the Dolomites, the sunny and wind-protected pistes make the Seiser Alm unique and incomparable to any other ski region in the Alps. The Seiser Alm is the paradise par excellence for winter sports of all kinds and for all age groups.

While at some ski resorts it is often necessary to take off your skis before every gondola ride, which results in long waiting times and crowds, on the Seiser Alm, you can keep your skis on. Skiers can save valuable time also because all the slopes that crossed the roads have been bridged by the new passages. This means that the connections between the ski runs can be used without any annoying loss of time. This means that the skiing day is fully utilised.

Together with Val Gardena, the Seiser Alm forms a ski area with 181 km of slopes and 79 lifts – making Seiser Alm/Val Gardena the biggest ski area in the Dolomites! Adventurous ski tours such as the Seiser Alm Ronda, the Seiser Alm/Val Gardena Ronda and the Sellaronda take you on a day trip through the Dolomites.
