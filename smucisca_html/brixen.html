An insane amount on offer in the SkiWelt area

The SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser - Brixental is one of the largest, most modern and most ecological ski areas in the world. 81 modern lifts, over 275 kilometres of pistes and 80 huts bring variety and fun to your winter day.

Insanely large, insanely varied, insanely cosy: the SkiWelt combines the best of winter on over 275 kilometres of pistes - and is only an hour's drive from Salzburg, Innsbruck and Munich. 81 modern lifts lead uphill from the nine access points. A unique mountain panorama spreads out at the top, with views of the sublime Wilder Kaiser and over 70 three-thousand metre peaks up to the GroÃŸglockner. 80 rustic, family-run huts with sun terraces and service provide a popular Tyrolean refreshment stop in between.

Winter fun for every size
Whether beginner, family, sporty skier or leisure skier: SkiWelt has the right slopes for every type of skier. Families will find plenty of easy and insanely wide slopes as well as practice slopes, child-safe lifts and many special experiences such as 22 professional ski schools with their own children's ski schools as well as mini and toddler care on the mountain from the age of one. All pistes are insanely wide and are perfectly groomed every day. Whether blue, red or black pistes, everyone can choose their piste according to ability and enjoyment, even if they have different levels of skiing ability, and at the end you can meet up again for the onward journey. Safety first: SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser - Brixental. skiwelt.at/families

Variety from early to late
For all those who love variety, there is something new to discover every day on over 275 kilometres of pistes. Everyone can choose their route every day so that no piste has to be skied twice, unless of course you want to. The SkiWelt Tour or even the KitzSkiWelt Tour, the longest ski circuit in the world, await sporty, frequent skiers. Ski until your calves burn: Off you go to 20 insanely long valley runs. For adventure-seekers, there are three fun and snow parks with different levels of difficulty, ski movie, race and speed measuring tracks as well as Austria's largest night skiing area in SÃ¶ll from Wednesday to Saturday. From the end of January, SkiWelt will extend its opening hours in the afternoon for even longer fun on the slopes, as it does every year. This is an annual service provided by SkiWelt at no extra charge. From 16 February, SkiWelt Brixen and SkiWelt Westendorf will also be taking all early risers to the slopes every Friday and Saturday from 7.30 am at no extra cost. Even longer skiing fun for the same price. skiwelt.at

No piste twice on the SkiWelt Tour
On the SkiWelt Tour, skiers and snowboarders discover the best aspects of one of the largest, most modern and most ecological ski areas in the world. Depending on the direction of travel, there are 69 to 84 kilometres of pistes between Brixen im Thale, Ellmau, Going, Hopfgarten, Itter, Scheffau, SÃ¶ll and Westendorf. The
SkiWelt Tour can be skied in one or more days and can be started and finished in any village. And the best thing is: no lift or piste has to be skied twice, unless you want to. In addition, a SkiWelt hut with regional specialities invites you to stop off every 3.5 km. skiwelt.at/skiwelttour

Be sure to make a note: The longest ski circuit in the world
You should have skied the KitzSkiWelt Tour at least once in your life: The longest ski circuit in the world combines the SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser - Brixental and the neighbouring KitzSki ski area. The 88 kilometres of downhill runs, on which 17,000 metres of altitude difference are covered, stretch from Tyrol to Salzburg. This mega ski experience can also start and end in any town. The longest variant leads from Going to Hollersbach and back. The tour can be completed in one or more days. There is also a ski bus connection from Pass Thurn back to Brixental Valley. Whether the KitzSkiWelt Tour ticket as a day ticket or the SuperSkiCard for multi-day tickets - all guests have a wide range of options to customise this very special experience. www.kitzskiwelt.at

Insanely eventful - on and off the piste
Three fun parks, speed and ski movie and race tracks get the best out of a day's skiing in the SkiWelt Wilder Kaiser - Brixental. Children are eager to take part in a raven rally, a bewitched children's birthday party or winter night witchcraft in SkiWelt SÃ¶ll. Don't miss: The best photo spots in the AlpenigluÂ® village in SkiWelt Brixen, at the gourmet evening in the champagne gondola of the Hexenwasser gondola lift or at Austria's highest pilgrimage church on the Hohe Salve. And one of 80 rustic, family-run huts with delicious Tyrolean cuisine every 3.5 kilometres of pistes. skiwelt.at
